3. Scope

In Scope

Out of Scope

The product will provide an interactive  system that will be user-friendly and  straightforward. It will not require any  training on how to use the application.

If the user has some kind of disability  or mental impairment, this would be  beyond of the abilities of the  

application.

The product will help those people who  do not know what to do when they are  in some kind of mood.

Food and medical advice will not be  included in the project.

4. Deliverables

This project will deliver the following.

Deliverable

Description

A mobile app

The application will be available for  Android and iOS.

Mood selection

The user will be able to select multiple  moods that they associate to at that  moment.

Questionnaire

If the user does not identify with any of  the presented moods, he will be able to  answer a few questions, do the  application can designate the user’s  mood.

Activities suggestions

The application will suggest various  activities to the user, according to the  chosen mood(s).

5. Assumptions

This project makes the following assumptions;

- The project will remain mostly focuesed on the creation of a mobile app. Any  changes to the format of the deliverable will unlikly not change throughout the life  of the product.

- The team will remain consistent from beginning of project begining to the end of  the project with no members leaving or entering during its length.

- The overall project document and deliverable will likely go through several  revisions before the final document is chosen and submitted.

- No substantial increase in budget will be put towards the project and the team  will make do with what equipment they have available.
