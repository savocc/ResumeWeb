About the company:

Fantastic Five is based in Toronto and other cities around the world, the team develops and  maintains web applications.

About the project:

Mind Manager is an application for a mobile phone, in which the user selects information or  answers questions on their mood and how they are feeling. After which the app will give a  series of options the user can select such as activities, music, poetry, and general locations  that would best suit them in their current state of mood.
